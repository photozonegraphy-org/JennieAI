Project Timeline

What to Expect, Month by Month

Most projects take 1 to 3 months. Here's a realistic picture of what that journey looks like — no sugarcoating.

W1
Week 1 — Discovery

Discovery Call & Proposal

We meet via Zoom or Google Meet to understand your brand, audience, goals, and budget. Within a few days you receive a detailed written proposal with scope, timeline, and itemised pricing — no obligation to proceed.

W2
Week 2–3 — Planning

Agreement, Content Collection & Domain Setup

Once you're happy with the proposal, we agree on terms. We collect your photos, bio, service details, and any brand guidelines. Domain and hosting decisions are made — all invoices go to your email first.

M1
Month 1 — Design

Design Mockups & Your Feedback

We build out the visual design — layouts, colour palette, typography, and gallery structure — and share it with you for review. We revise based on your feedback before writing a single line of code.

M2
Month 2 — Build

Development, Content Population & Testing

We build the approved design into a fully functional website, populate it with all your content, and test across devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile — as well as multiple browsers.

M3
Month 3 — Launch

Final Review, Launch & Handover

You review the completed site on a staging environment. After final approvals, we launch and hand over all credentials, files, and access. The site is fully yours from this moment on.
